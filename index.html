How I Work

I have a decent amount of experience when it comes to UI design! Although I’m still learning a lot, I follow a fairly organized approach. I usually start by laying everything out in Figma to plan the structure and visual style. Then I build the foundation using HTML, bring the design to life with CSS, and finally add interactivity using JavaScript.
